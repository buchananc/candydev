I'M CANDACE

FULL STACK WEB DESIGNER

I am a permanently curious DIYer that loves to make anything digital more eye-catching and easy to use. After creating a personal couponing blog in 2003, I started teaching myself HTML/CSS. I quickly realized that I enjoyed coding and building websites. I began looking at what other people had built and thoroughly analyzed the source code to deconstruct it and learn how to do it myself.  
I have been extremely honored to share my passion for programming, tinkering, making and engineering with thousands of students as an Elementary Technology Teacher.  This opportunity led me to complete a Coding Bootcamp and now I am ready to design and code beautifully simple things.
